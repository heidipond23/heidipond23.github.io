Design Principles

White Space

Sunnyside Gardens

This local website is for information as well as shopping. White space is used between the three horizontally aligned icons to clarify the sections of the website and guide the user to the most useful pages.

sunnysidegardens.net

Visual Heirarchy

Grub Street

Grub Street uses visual heirarchy to lead the reader to the most important information. First, the image, then the catching headline, next the name of the website, and the word “New York” which gives context to the article.

grubstreet.com

Contrast

Ezuhaib.com

Ezuhaib is a psychology blog that uses contrast to invoke a specific feeling. The black background is a stark canvas for light and bright pictures. The reader has an emotional experience with the picture before even reading the title of the article.

ezuhaib.com
