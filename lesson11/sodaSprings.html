Leech Problems

A series of unfortunate events has taken place in Soda Springs over the last few weeks. Tourist coming to collect soda water have been bombarded by leeches in the water.

Rather than forego the activity, people have found that kicking their legs high in the water while they walk prevents the leeches from having time to latch onto their feet, ancles, and legs.
