Park Runners Complain

Runners in the Preston City Park complain about the lack of "Hit and Run" incidents; or maybe they mean "Run and get Hit on". Many young women run at the park with hopes of finding a future companion, or even just a date for the weekend. They voice their frustration to city officials who respond with, "We can't force the boys to ask you out."
