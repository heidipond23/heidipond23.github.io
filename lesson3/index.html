Site Purpose

The purpose of Weather or Not is to provide easy to navigate access to the current weather conditions and forecast in Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven, Idaho. The site includes a Storm center as well as a photo gallery. The viewer will find access to current/relevant town information and articles. There will be a link to major news articles.
