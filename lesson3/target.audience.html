Target Audience

This webpage should be easily navigated and accessed by anyone, anywhere. Anyone ages 10-90 will find what they are looking for on whatever device they have. The responsive navigtaion and media queries make navigating the site easy from a child's tablet to a grandmother's new and unfamilar iphone. The content will be acurate, updated, and appropriate.

Persona 1 - Nico

Job Title:

Yoga Instructor / Barista / Hipster

Demographics

23 years old

Lives in Brooklyn, NY

Single

Estranged from family

Working on degree in art and humanities

Goals and Tasks:

Nico is socially driven. He keeps his laptop with him at all times to keep track of social media updates and update his status. Fashion is his passion. He keeps the weather and forcast app open at all times in order to plan his outfits. He depends on being able to quickly navigate web pages in order to get back to tweeting.

Persona 2 - Valerie

Job Title:

Elementary School Secretary / Grandma

Demographics

62 years old

Lives in Idaho Falls, ID

Married 40 years

Grandma of 14

Spends free time with family

Goals and Tasks:

Valerie is a conservative hard worker. She feels insecure on a computer because she remembers the time when there weren't computers in the home. She is proficient on Word. She checks the weather on her phone when planning upcoming activities with her family. She is easily frustrated when a website is difficult to navigate or requires any technical savy.

Quote

"A garden of love grows in a grandmother's heart."

Scenarios

1. A little girl is hoping to play outside in the water but is only allowed once it's 80 degree. She checks the site on her tablet every 15 minutes for the updated temperature.

2. Two grumpy old men want to go fishing for the weekend but only if the weather is fair. They check the website for an accurate forecast.

3. A teenage boy has to do a report on Preston, Idaho. He wants to know how the weather compares to Idaho Falls and what kind of events are happening there.

4. A family wants to travel to Eastern Idaho for a reunion but can't decide which city. They visit the site to compare the weather in each city

5. A husband in the dog house needs to plant a flower garden for his wife. He needs to know how windy it will be before making plans to plant.
